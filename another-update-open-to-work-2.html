March 5, 2026

OK, I promise I'll stop posting these #open-to-work updates (soon).

On January 1st, CommunicateHealth — the company I worked for since late 2017 — merged with Lumina Corps. This was not supposed to be a big change. My job title, list of responsibilities, clients, and projects all stayed the same. Unfortunately, it quickly became clear that Lumina Corps and I were not a good fit. So, in early March we parted ways.

Right now, I'm taking a bit of time to make sure whatever I pursue next is a good fit for my interests, talents, and experience.

If you think you might have an opportunity that matches, please find me on LinkedIn, I'd love to discuss!
